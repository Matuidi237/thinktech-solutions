Among the projects closest to our hearts, the **Bouka Creativ' Learning** platform holds a special place. Built in partnership with the Boukarou incubator, it pursues an ambitious goal: supporting the upskilling of 42,000 young people across Cameroon's 10 regions.

## The challenge: training at scale, all across Cameroon

When Boukarou reached out to us, the need was clear but demanding: a platform able to host a massive number of learners, across urban and rural areas alike, with widely varying levels of internet access. This wasn't just about putting courses online: it meant designing an experience that was genuinely accessible to a very diverse youth population.

## Our approach

We structured the project around three priorities:

1. **Performance on limited connections**: every page was optimized to load fast even on 3G, with particular attention paid to image and video weight.
2. **Ease of use**: sign-up, navigating between modules, and progress tracking had to be understandable without any prior training.
3. **Robustness at scale**: the infrastructure had to absorb spikes of simultaneous connections without any drop in service quality.

## The results

Today, Bouka Creativ' Learning is fully operational and continues to welcome new learners every week. Beyond the numbers, it's the real-world impact that drives us: young people from regions often underserved by traditional training offerings now have access to quality learning paths, without needing to travel.

>

## What this project taught us

Working at this scale confirmed something we believe deeply: a platform designed for the African context (mobile-first, light on data, simple to use) genuinely changes the game for access to training. It's this approach we now apply to every new project, regardless of its size.
